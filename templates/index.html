About: Apple Picker

Having trouble reaching a consensus? ApplePicker serves as a simple polling system while weighing in additional information, like how much a decision matters to someone. ApplePicker serves as the perfect medium for making the choices we don't want to put too much effort in but care enough to not rely on a very basic voting system.

Help: Instructions

Creating a Group

Simply have the host click a button and set the polling settings. Then share the unique key to others you want to participate.

Joining a Group

You can either directly enter the URL or type the unique key into the website. Simply click the button to join a group and enter.

Start the Voting

You can set deadlines for decisions that need to be made sooner. Each decision can also have weights for those who really want something.
